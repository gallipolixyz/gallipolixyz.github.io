What is the Gallipoli community?

The Gallipoli community is a learning and sharing platform for students and mentors interested in web security. Our primary goal is to provide accelerated web security education and assist participants in finding vulnerabilities.

Who can join the community?

The community is primarily aimed at students, but experienced professionals interested in mentoring are also welcome. Anyone willing to support students can participate in our community.

How can I register?

Registration is currently closed. However, when new memberships open up, you can register through our website.

What activities can I participate in at Gallipoli?

In our community, you can access educational materials, take vulnerability finding tasks, share experiences with other participants, and engage in mentoring.

How does mentoring work?

Mentors provide guidance and answer questions for students. Those interested in mentoring can introduce themselves in the relevant section after registering with the community.

How does the education process work?

The education process involves weekly tasks. Participants learn about specific topics and complete related assignments. They earn points based on their achievements.

How does the point system work?

Participants earn points by completing tasks. These points help prioritize their contributions within our community.

What are the participation requirements?

Active participation, timely task completion, and interaction with other members are some of the basic requirements.

Is the Gallipoli community free?

Yes, joining and benefiting from the Gallipoli community is free of charge. You can access all educational materials and activities without any cost.

How can I contact you if I have more questions?

If you have further questions, you can reach us through our social media accounts.
